About the data

There are {{countOL | numeral("0,0") }} records and 9 fields in this register.

Domain names and trading names are provided by the operator. The Gambling Commission cannot guarantee the accuracy of information provided by third parties.

The 9 fields in this register

Field name

Field description

Account number

The unique reference for the operator.

Name

The name of the operator.

Status

The status of the operator account.

Head office address

The head office address for the operator.

Activities

The current activities the operator is licensed to operate under.

Sanctions

Details of regulatory sanctions imposed on the operator by the Gambling Commission.

Actions

Details of actions taken by the Gambling Commission against the operator. These are not sanctions.

Domain names

The domain names the operator has registered to provide online gambling activities through.

Trading names

The trading names the operator trades under
