About the data

There are 15 fields in the dataset.

For accurate premises information, you should contact the relevant Licensing Authority, as they maintain an accurate and up-to-date register for their local area.

The 15 fields in this register

Field name

Field description

Account_Number

The unique reference for the licence holder.

Account_Name

The name of the licence holder.

Trading Names

The trading names the licence holder trades under. This is only applicable to operator accounts.

Applicant_Property_name

The name of the property the Operator operates the business from.

Applicant_Address1

The first line of the Operator's address.

Applicant_City

The city the Operator is based in.

Applicant_Postcode

The postcode of the address the Operator is based in.

premises_Address1

The first line of the licensed premises address.

premises_Address2

The second line of the licensed premises address.

premises_City

The city the licensed premises address is in.

premises_Postcode

The postcode of the licensed premises address.

StatusDate

The date associated with the current status of the licence.

Activity

The type of activity associated with the premises.

Local_Authority_Name

The licensing authority responsible for issuing the licence.

LicenceStatus

The status of the licence associated with the premises.
